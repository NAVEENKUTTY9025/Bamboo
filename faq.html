Your Questions, Answered.

Everything you need to know about bamboo clothing, sustainability, shipping, and caring for your garments.

Bamboo Essence is a sustainable fashion brand dedicated to creating high-quality apparel and accessories from organic bamboo fibers. We focus on ethical production, eco-friendly packaging, and carbon-neutral operations.
Our bamboo fabric is sourced from FSC-certified bamboo groves in Southeast Asia, and all garments are ethically manufactured in WRAP-certified facilities in Vietnam and China. We visit each factory regularly to ensure fair wages and safe conditions.
We are currently pursuing B Corp certification and expect to complete the process by 2026. Meanwhile, we follow B Corp standards for social and environmental performance.
Yes! Our flagship Eco-Hub is located in Portland, Oregon at 245 Greenway Ave. We also have seasonal pop-ups in Seattle and San Francisco. Visit our Contact page for hours.
Bamboo grows rapidly without pesticides, requires 70% less water than cotton, and absorbs 5x more CO2. It also regenerates from its own roots, eliminating the need for replanting.
Yes! Our bamboo fabric is 100% biodegradable and will naturally decompose in soil conditions within 1-2 years, leaving no microplastics behind.
We calculate the carbon footprint of every shipment and invest in verified reforestation and renewable energy projects to offset those emissions completely.
Absolutely. All our mailers are made from 100% recycled materials and are home-compostable. Shipping boxes are FSC-certified and plastic-free.
Bamboo fabric is incredibly soft — often compared to cashmere or silk. It's breathable, hypoallergenic, and naturally moisture-wicking, making it perfect for sensitive skin.
We offer unisex sizing from XS to 3XL. Our bamboo fabric has a slight stretch, and we provide detailed size charts on each product page. If between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a relaxed fit.
Yes, all our bamboo fiber is organically grown without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers. We also use OEKO-TEX certified dyes that are free from harmful chemicals.
Our accessory line includes bamboo sunglasses, reusable straws, phone cases, eco-totes, and bamboo-fiber socks. Each item is designed to be plastic-free and functional.
US Admin Dashboard typically arrive within 3-7 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout.
Yes! We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination — calculated at checkout.
We offer free returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags. Start a return through your Dashboard or contact support.
We're sorry! Please email photos of the damage to support@bambooessence.com within 7 days, and we'll send a replacement or full refund immediately.
Machine wash cold with like colors on a gentle cycle. Use mild, eco-friendly detergent. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners as they can break down bamboo fibers.
We recommend line drying or tumble drying on low heat. High heat may cause shrinkage. Bamboo dries quickly due to its breathable nature.
Quality bamboo fabric resists pilling. Our garments are made with long-staple fibers that maintain smoothness. Following care instructions will keep them pill-free for years.
Spot clean gently with mild soap. For tougher stains, soak in cold water with baking soda before washing. Avoid harsh stain removers.
